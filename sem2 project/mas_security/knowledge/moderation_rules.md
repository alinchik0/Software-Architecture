# Platform Moderation Policy
- Phishing: Links mimicking official domains, requests for credentials, shortened URLs in DMs.
- Toxicity: Slurs, hate speech, targeted harassment, doxxing.
- Spam: Repetitive posting, unsolicited promotions, bot-like syntax patterns.
- ALLOWED: Standard URLs to reputable domains (github.com, official docs, known brands), constructive feedback, normal login activity, educational links.
- False Positives: Constructive criticism, news sharing, verified brand mentions, standard verification emails from known providers.
